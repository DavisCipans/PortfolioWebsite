Get to know me..

my name is Davis!

Based in Copenhagen but originally hailed from small coastal city of Liepaja, Latvia.
I focus on delivering great User Experience through user-centered design approach. Currently working at OrderYOYO, where I helped the company reach out to more potential clients through their website and established consistent, business orientated visual identity.

As of now I am working with a Business to Customer Web team in order to completely overhaul User Interface of partner websites by using the guidelines of Google’s Material Design system and improve food ordering experience, while learning more about Lean-UX methodology.

My interest for graphic design was born from my love for video games.
Before starting Multimedia Design & Communication studies, I volunteered for TeamLiquid’s community site. There I learned to manage my time and work in teams.

Positive feedback from the journalism artworks I created, pushed me to move forward in the creative field. Journey in which I have always maintained strong attention to detail and willingness to constantly improve myself as a professional and a person.
