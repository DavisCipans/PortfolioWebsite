The challenge

Redesign existing business to business website to increase intake leads and engage with potential partners. In order to increase sales of product from the website.

  • Design Strategy
  • User Experience

Duration

About OrderYOYO

Established in 2013, OrderYOYO is a white label, startup company that provides restaurants and takeaways with their own takeaway system platform. OrderYOYO provides restaurant with a web page, and apps both for Android and Apple devices, allowing consumers to order directly from restaurant through an app or a website with no extra charges.

Old website: Research and discovery.

Trough user interviews and think aloud tests while using the website, we were able to determine problems to an existing website and reasons why people would sign up for the project and discover flaws that would deter them from engaging with the company.

Not only the content was important to people, but also overall look of the website, the design language and color usage. That would all highly contribute to the trustworthiness factor.

Competitors: Market and Qualitative research.

To acknowledge benefits and pros that potential customers would receive from signing up for OrderYOYO’s product, we conducted interviews with current customers and did market research. Interviewing existing customers helped us understand better the benefits they truly cared about and the ones we should showcase. And they gave additional time to gather feedback to continually improve the product itself. Win-win situation!

Competitor research was done in order to understand what information and features are commonly showcased, how other companies had tackled the problem of structuring their content. And how they would pursue potential customers to sign up for services they offer.

Based on our research, conclusions were made to keep OrderYOYO’s website simple.
Efficiently show features that the company offers and familiarize potential customers with the company and to create welcoming and positive image.

Designing a new website: Creating a visual identity.

Whilst company had logo, there was no uniting visual identity to send a clear message. Part of my job when designing a website was creating a visual identity that would portray OrderYOYO as trustworthy, serious and businesslike company for customers. While retaining peaceful and kind image. That was done by using already established YOYO Green color and additional ocean-dark shade of blue.

User centered: User friendly website

To create efficient dialogue between the company and users, website had to be simple to use and had to display clearly understandable information. To achieve that User-centered design framework of process was used. Where trough vigorous prototyping and user testing cycles we were able to come up with design that would achieve the goals we set.

Quick win features were implemented on the old website, while new one was being developed. Like Support chat plugin, that would allow both customers of OrderYOYO and restaurant platform customers to reach OrderYOYO’s support much quicker and easier.

Feature section

User friendly way to showcase large feature set the company has to offer.
No loading pages, every detail is one click away.

Calculator

Displaying one of the main benefits of joining OrderYOYO - no fees per order, in a interactive way.

Testimonials

Letting successful customers tell the story.
Deepen the trust by showcasing things that people they can relate to have told.
